RUNGTA INTERNATIONAL SKILLS UNIVERSITY

Established in 1999 under the GDR Educational Society for Higher & Technical Education by Shri Ghanshyam Dasji Rungta, Rungta College began as a single engineering institute. Under the visionary leadership of Group Chairman Santosh Rungta, it expanded into a renowned educational group comprising four colleges in Engineering, Pharmacy, Commerce, and Science. In 2025, with official government approval, it transformed into Rungta International University—Central India’s first private skill-focused university. Located on an 80-acre campus in Bhilai, it offers NAAC A Grade accredited, industry-aligned UG, PG, and doctoral programs across disciplines with global certifications from Google, Microsoft, and IBM.

RUNGTA GROUP Leadership With technology changing the way every man lives his life, from moment to moment it has become imperative that society moves strongly towards education. Not in the traditional way, but using the same technologies knowledge that is suddenly so very accessible. I strongly believe that our collective future lies in the hands of the most intelligent and well articulated new generation and the difference between any two human beings will be only defined by education or the lack of it. Our future will be defined and decided by another generation. Hence, it is of supreme importance that this generation of young people, our children, are equipped to take those decisions on our behalf.
